Feel good about your work

Mozilla has big ambitions for the next decade to build impactful products that are different — that have more respect for the individuals using them, that operate with a different business model, and that explore new forms of openness. It’s going to take hard work that Mozilla is uniquely suited to take on. It’s why we’re here. It’s who we are. And it’s our future.

Bring your drive, your creativity, your big ideas and your new perspectives to make the difference we’re aiming for.

Our values bring us together as one team

How we work together is as important as what we make together. In 2022, we finalized a yearlong effort to codify what helps our team be its best, and we love where we landed. Our shared operating values drive confident decision-making, boost healthy collaborations and just plain make it easier to get stuff done.

    {% call picto( base_el='li', title='We welcome differences', image_url='protocol/img/icons/brand/orange/speedometer.svg', body=True, ) %}

    Because we want Mozilla to be a place where everyone can do their best work, and because we know diverse perspectives and experiences result in better solutions, we welcome differences.

    {% endcall %} {% call picto( base_el='li', title='We are relationship-minded', image_url='protocol/img/icons/brand/orange/feature-extensions-puzzle.svg', body=True, ) %}

    We take the long view of our connections and interactions, both with each other and with the people who use our products. This requires a commitment to empathy, active listening and giving without the expectation of getting. It’s digging in to understand what makes people tick and caring about that.

    {% endcall %} {% call picto( base_el='li', title='We practice responsible participation', image_url='protocol/img/icons/brand/orange/feature-extensions-puzzle.svg', body=True, ) %}

    Transparent and open processes are a unique part of our genetic code. When coupled with responsible participation — being generous and kind with both our inputs and our outputs — we create trust and accountability and elevate the quality of our decisions.

    {% endcall %} {% call picto( base_el='li', title='We have grit', image_url='protocol/img/icons/brand/orange/feature-extensions-puzzle.svg', body=True, ) %}

    We are here to do hard things, take on giants and make a difference in people’s online lives — so we must have grit. Having grit means facing obstacles with optimism, determination to find new ways forward and being relentless about learning from what worked and what didn’t.

    {% endcall %}
